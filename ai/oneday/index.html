The Program

Five phases.
One outcome.

The program runs in parallel with construction — beginning 3–6 months before Occupancy Permit and culminating in a single, hospitality-grade Turnover Day.

Phase 01
QC Program Launch
T-minus 90–120 days

Procore QC Coordinator Standards
Phase 02
Trade Final Finish Inspections
T-minus 60–90 days

After Turnover Day

Aria by Chassis Labs.

The moment keys change hands, a new operational layer begins. Aria is Chassis Labs' flagship AI agent for homeowner warranty and customer service — purpose-built for BC real estate developers. It handles the post-closing service layer so your HOC team doesn't have to.

01
Warranty Inquiry Handling
Aria manages inbound homeowner warranty requests 24/7 across chat, email, phone, and SMS — resolving common inquiries instantly and routing complex claims to the right contact without delay.
02
2-5-10 Claim Triage
Aria classifies every inbound claim against BC's 2-5-10 warranty program, ensuring the correct coverage period is applied and the right builder or trade is engaged — automatically and on record.
03
Trade Callback Scheduling
Warranty trade visits are scheduled directly through Aria — homeowners book their own appointments, confirmations are automated, and the developer maintains a full audit trail without manual coordination.
04
Builder Routing & Escalation
Issues that fall outside standard warranty scope are routed to the right team member instantly. Aria monitors open items, flags delays, and escalates before homeowners have to follow up a second time.

Optional Engagement Layer

Purpose-Built AI
for the Turnover Runway.

For clients who want to go further during the program itself, Chassis Labs designs and deploys bespoke AI capabilities tailored to the turnover engagement. Scoped per project and activated when you're ready.

01
Predictive Deficiency Modelling
Historical deficiency data by trade, unit type, and floor is analyzed to predict where issues are most likely to emerge — so inspectors prioritize high-risk areas before they become schedule problems.
02
Photo-Based QC Review
Site reps upload unit photos at gate inspections. An AI model cross-references images against acceptance criteria, flagging finish deviations before a human inspector walks the floor — reducing inspection time and eliminating subjectivity.
03
Completion Velocity Forecasting
A real-time model tracks unit completion rates against the Turnover Day target and surfaces early warnings when the schedule is at risk — accounting for trade velocity, deficiency close-out rates, and inspection throughput.
04
Homeowner Communication Automation
AI-drafted, personalized closing communications and appointment confirmations triggered automatically by Juniper milestone completions — tailored to each homeowner's unit, timeline, and slot.
05
Post-Turnover Analytics
Deficiency pattern analysis generates trade performance scorecards — close-out rates, repeat item categories, average resolution times by trade. A data-backed basis for future procurement and continuous improvement.
